10 Compliance Tasks Every MSME Must Do (2025)

A quick Delhi-NCR checklist to avoid penalties and keep payroll & taxes clean.

Who is this for: MSME owners/finance leads in Delhi-NCR who want a crisp, penalty-free compliance routine.

How to use it: Tick what’s done, flag gaps, and plan the next 30 days. If you’re unsure, we’ll review it with you on a short call.

Your 10 Tasks

  1. GST Registration & correct HSN/SAC mapping
  2. Monthly/Quarterly GST Returns (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B) and payment on time
  3. GST Reconciliation (2A/2B vs purchase register) to prevent ITC loss
  4. TDS deduction, deposit & quarterly returns; Form 16/16A issuance
  5. Income Tax Return (ITR) filing and advance/self-assessment tax tracking
  6. Payroll processing with pay heads, reimbursements & final settlements
  7. PF & ESI registrations, monthly challans and returns; UAN/ESIC updates
  8. Maintenance of books of account & monthly MIS for decision-making
  9. Statutory registers & basic labour law compliance (wage, leave, bonus)
  10. Document management: invoices, agreements, vendor KYC, and audit trail
